J-Crossover: Sixth-Man Extraordinaire

Jamal Crawford was everything that any coach would want off the bench. One of the cleanest ball handlers of the millenium, J-Crossover brought streetball flashiness to the NBA, with incredible handles, long range, and a high-arching jump shot that looked like it might hit the jumbotron.

He won Sixth-Man of the Year honors twice while in a Clippers uniform, a testament to his impact off the bench. Whether it was four-point plays, dishing out assists, or throwing lobs to Blake Griffin, Jamal Crawford was a massive fan favorite for the franchise and gave some of his best years to Los Angeles.
